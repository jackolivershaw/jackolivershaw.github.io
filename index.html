Jack Shaw

Doctoral student at Yale University, studying paleobiology.

I am broadly interested in the macroecological trends described by the fossil record. Specifically, I am investigating how network analysis can elucidate our understanding of community structure and how it has changed throughout the phanerozoic. I am also working on projects involving the effects of hyperthermals on symbiont-bearing planktonic foraminifera, the taphonomy of Marrellomorphs from the Fezouata Formations of Morocco, and sexual selection in Ostracoda.
